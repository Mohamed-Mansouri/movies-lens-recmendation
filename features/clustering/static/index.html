Pipeline ✕

Features (42 per user)

  • user_mean, user_count, user_std
  • 19 genre watch-share values
  • 20 SVD latent dimensions

All standardised with StandardScaler.

K-Means

Searches K=2–10, picks best silhouette score. Every user is assigned exactly one cluster.

DBSCAN

Density-based. eps auto-tuned from 90th pct of 5-NN distances. Sparse users are labelled noise (−1).

Radar Chart

Shows your genre watch-share (purple) vs. the cluster average (teal) across the 8 most distinctive genres in your cluster.

Cluster Recommendations

Top movies loved by your cluster members that you haven’t rated yet. Scored by average rating × log(number of raters) — high-rated and popular within the group.
